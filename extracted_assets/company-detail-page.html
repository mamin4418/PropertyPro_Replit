Implementation Notes:

  • The company detail page should be accessible by clicking on a company from the companies list.
  • Multiple tabs allow for organized access to company information, properties, contacts, documents, and notes.
  • The header includes quick action buttons for editing and deleting the company, as well as generating reports.
  • The stats cards display key metrics related to the company's properties and financial performance.
  • The properties section shows a preview of all properties owned by the company with quick statistics.
  • The Contacts tab should display a list of contacts associated with the company.
  • The Documents tab should display a list of documents related to the company.
  • The Notes tab should allow for adding and editing notes about the company.
  • Edit and Delete actions should include confirmation dialogs.
  • Property cards should be clickable and navigate to the property detail page.
